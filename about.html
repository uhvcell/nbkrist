About UHV Cell

Journey towards Holistic Human Development

Our Vision

The UHV Cell aims to foster a deep understanding of human values among students and faculty through self-exploration, reflection, and practice. It supports creating a harmonious society by integrating values into education and nurturing responsible, ethical, and compassionate individuals.

"Existence is Co-existence"

Key Objectives

  • To inculcate universal human values like truth, love, peace, responsibility, and justice.
  • To encourage self-reflection and self-awareness through dialogue-based learning.
  • To promote ethical and moral development among students and staff.
  • To integrate values into curriculum, conduct, and campus culture.

Core Concepts

Self-Exploration

Understanding one's purpose, role in society, and inner harmony through deep reflection.

Harmony Levels

Achieving balance within the individual, with family / society, and with nature.

Responsible Living

Promoting a lifestyle based on need-based living, not greed-based consumption.

Why UHV is Essential?

Holistic Growth

Develop a comprehensive understanding of human reality and existence through self-exploration.

Harmony & Peace

Learn to live in harmony with oneself, family, society, and the natural environment.

Ethical Living

Cultivate values that lead to a fulfilling, meaningful, and responsible life.
