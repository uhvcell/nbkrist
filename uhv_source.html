UHV CELL

ABOUT UHV CELL


Theme: "Value-based Education for Holistic Human Development" The UHV Cell aims to foster a deep understanding of human values among students and faculty through self-exploration, reflection, and practice. It supports creating a harmonious society by integrating values into education and nurturing responsible, ethical, and compassionate individuals.

Objectives of the UHV Cell:


• To inculcate universal human values like truth, love, peace, responsibility, and justice.
• To encourage self-reflection and self-awareness through dialogue-based learning.
• To promote ethical and moral development among students and staff.
• To integrate values into curriculum, conduct, and campus culture.
• To help students understand the purpose of education and align their personal and professional goals with societal well-being and sustainable living.

Key Activities of the UHV Cell:


• Organizing UHV Workshops (Level 1 and 2) for faculty and students.
• Conducting value education sessions and interactive group discussions.
• Hosting talks by value education experts and resource persons.
• Coordinating with AICTE and other institutions for UHV training and implementation.
• Maintaining a resource center with UHV-related books, videos, and materials.

Core Concepts Promoted:


• Holistic Human Development: Focus on physical, emotional, intellectual, and spiritual growth.
• Self-Exploration: Understand one's purpose, role in society, and inner harmony.
• Harmony at All Levels:
 Within the individual
 With family and society
 With nature and the environment
• Responsible Living: Promote a lifestyle based on need-based living, not greed-based consumption.
• Right Understanding & Relationships: Building mutual respect, trust, and responsibility.
